Home Cell Fellowship

Home Cell Fellowship Groups are te fabric of the church and the most important aspect of te home cell groups is the Spiritual Dynamism. People should be taught the cells are avenues for worship, learn, relate, win souls and experience the miraculous powers of God

The goal is to help people to personally encounter the supernatural God. HCF should be a place whee miracles occur on regular basis and people's problems are solved from the divine perspective. Cell meetings in Zion should spend less time in fellowship and more in evangelism. Cell lay-ministers should be active homemakers, searching for and helping people with problems, especially in that neighborhood. Cells in Zion may find it important to rotate the location of cell meetings in the participating homes. Wen Christians worship in a home, such home is blessed by God; therefore, it should be regarded a privilege to host a cell fellowship.
