The Story Of Zion Faith Mission

The vision began in 1989 i Maiduguri between Brother Abel Adeleke and Sister Oluremi Alabi, as they were planning to become married couple someday in the future. As we know, a noble purpose inspires sacrifice, stimulate innovation and encourages perseverance. The vision was kept close to our hearts. We received some theoretical training, we worked with Foursquare Nigeria to gain requisite experience, we prayed as required, and we consulted many church leaders for counseling.

Our Journey Here...

At the fullness of time, Zion began in 2008 at Peace House. Tim Arolayo was the resident pastor then. Isaiah Akinola was the director of Home Mission and Revival. Olusegun Ogunniyi was in charge of Prayer Ministry. Florence Olujobi was the Director of Finance. Oladele Albert was the Director of Mission and Evangelism. Adeshina Afolayan became the Director of Christian Education. Oluremi Adeleke became the Provost of Zion College of Theology.
