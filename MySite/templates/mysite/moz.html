We were inspired in the year 2013 to purchase a Zion City of Prayer. We prayerfully acted on this divine prompting, mobilizing human and material resources available to procure a landed property to build the city. A search party was raised and a place was secured at Akinwaare Village. The village is located Off Ido-Eruwa road in Ido Local Government Area of Oyo State.

The purchasing sum was transferred to Baba Baale Akinwaare's bank account as the total sum for the procurement of the 8 acres of land. Land Agreement document was prepared by Barrister Babatolu, and he also supervised the signing of the document. The survey plan was given to a competent surveyor and red copy must be provided to indicate official approval of the state government. Video coverage was done to record every stage of the procurement.

The camp might be divided into three developmental areas with the large auditorium at the centre. Residential portion should allow land lease to people who may wish to develop personal chalets. There should be a portion designated for the Secretariat and Administration.

  • There should be a well spacious prayer house, furnished with cubicles for personal prayers and meditations.
  • There should be a portion for recreational activities and relaxation centre.
  • There should be a central kitchen where all cooking can take place. This may be furnished with a moderately large dining hall.

The leadership of the church will need to go and pray over the land as soon as the 2013 Convention Program is over. After which a consultant will be hired to do the mapping of the campground.

We need a camp manager who can do it as a voluntary assignment. His duty is to visit and supervise whatever development going on at the camp. He might be entitled to whatever means of transporting himself to the camp. We need the camp board for the developmental plan and mapping.

Zion Camp Development Plan

  • The camp might be divided into three developmental areas with te large auditorium at the center.
  • Residential portion should allow land lease to people who may wish to develop personal chalets.
  • There should be a portion designated for the secretariat and administration.
  • We need a central borehole that would be located and should be able to supply waters to the campers
  • We need to facilitate electricity transformer that can supply electricity to the campground. It should be strategically located where it would not be vandalized.
  • There should be a well spacious prayer house, furnished with cubicles for personal prayer and meditation. This shall be called Zion Prayer Mountain.
  • There should be a portion for recreational activities and relaxation centre.
  • There should be a central kitchen where all cooking can take place. This may be furnished with a moderately large dining hall.
  • There should be a camp library, book store, mini market and health centre.
  • We shall need 5 very large common rooms for male adults, female adults, male and female youths and children.
  • There should be a residential area where people can build chalets.
  • There should be a central administration building with offices and conference rooms.
